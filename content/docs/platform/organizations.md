---

An Organization on Pixel represents a company, lab, or group rather than an individual user.

Organizations can:

- Own Projects and Models
- Contain multiple [Teams](/docs/platform/teams)
- Manage member roles and permissions across all of their Projects
- Centralize billing and usage for the APIs and Platform

Organizations are the recommended way to collaborate at scale on Pixel — individual accounts are best suited for personal projects and experimentation, while Organizations are built for shared ownership.
