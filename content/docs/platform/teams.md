Teams sit inside an [Organization](/docs/platform/organizations) and group members around a specific set of Projects or responsibilities.

Typical uses of Teams include:

- Splitting a large Organization by product area (for example, a Models team and a Platform team)
- Scoping access so a Team only sees the Projects relevant to their work
- Assigning reviewers for Issues, Discussions, and Releases within a Project

Team membership determines what a user can do within the Projects a Team has access to, without requiring Organization-wide permissions.
