Pixel Platform is Inserloft's flagship product. It is not just a place to upload models — it sits at the intersection of three things developers already know:

- **GitHub** — for collaboration, versioning, and project structure.
- **Hugging Face** — for hosting, sharing, and discovering models and datasets.
- **AI Workspace** — a place to actually build with AI, not just store it.

On Pixel, users can:

- Create Projects
- Manage and version models
- Download and upload models
- Collaborate with teammates
- Share datasets
- Publish documentation

The core unit of organization on Pixel is the **Project** — see [Projects](/docs/platform/projects) for details on why Inserloft deliberately avoids calling them "repositories."
