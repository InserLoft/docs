Cleo has persistent memory across conversations, similar in spirit to ChatGPT's memory feature.

This means Cleo can:

- Recall relevant facts you've shared in previous sessions
- Maintain context about your preferences and ongoing projects
- Avoid repeating the same clarifying questions across sessions

Memory is designed to make Cleo feel like a continuous assistant rather than a series of disconnected chats — particularly important given Cleo's role as the central assistant across the rest of the Inserloft ecosystem, including the Pixel [Platform](/docs/platform/pixel).
