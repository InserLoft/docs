Cleo is Inserloft's AI assistant, and it is intended to be the center of the entire ecosystem — not just a chatbot bolted onto the Platform.

Cleo can help with:

- Programming
- Research
- Everyday tasks
- Documents
- Writing
- Open-ended conversation

Typical prompts look like:

- "Cleo, help me with my homework"
- "Cleo, research this topic"
- "Cleo, build me a website"
- "Cleo, explain this code"
- "Cleo, summarize this document"

Cleo is powered by Inserloft's [Kyro](/docs/models/kyro) frontier models and is exposed both as a product at chat.inserloft.dev-style surfaces and programmatically through the [Chat API](/docs/apis/chat-api) and [Responses API](/docs/apis/responses-api).
