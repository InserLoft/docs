Skills are packaged, reusable capabilities that extend what Cleo can do for a specific domain or workflow.

Where a [Tool](/docs/cleo/tools) is a single action Cleo can take, a Skill is a bundle of instructions, and sometimes tools, aimed at a specific kind of task — for example, a Skill for writing research summaries in a specific format, or one for scaffolding a particular type of project.

Skills are published and shared the same way other developer-facing artifacts are on [OpenGit](/docs/opengit/introduction), making it possible for the community to extend Cleo's capabilities without waiting on a core product release.
