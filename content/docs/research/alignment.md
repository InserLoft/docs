Alignment research at Inserloft focuses on making models behave in ways that are helpful, predictable, and safe across both the frontier [Kyro](/docs/models/kyro) family and smaller [Baby LLMs](/docs/models/baby-llms).

This includes work on instruction following, refusal behavior, and evaluation of failure modes documented in each model's [Model Card](/docs/models/model-cards). Alignment findings feed directly into how models like [Cleo](/docs/cleo/introduction) are trained and deployed as an assistant, since Cleo is one of the most consequential surfaces for model behavior.
