Inserloft Research publishes findings on architectures, training, alignment, inference, and agents as they mature.

Publications typically take one of a few forms:

- Technical reports accompanying a new model release, such as Kyro or Verty
- Focused write-ups on a specific technique explored internally
- Benchmark and evaluation methodology shared alongside the [Benchmarks](/docs/models/benchmarks) tracked for each model

Where relevant, publications are paired with open releases through [OpenGit](/docs/opengit/introduction) so the community can reproduce results directly.
