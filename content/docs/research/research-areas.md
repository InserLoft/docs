Inserloft Research is organized around a focused set of areas:

- AI and LLMs broadly
- Frontier model development, feeding directly into [Kyro](/docs/models/kyro)
- Model architectures
- Training methodology
- Alignment
- Inference efficiency
- Agents
- Distributed systems, which underpin both training at scale and serving models through the [Platform](/docs/platform/deployments)

Inserloft is also building an internal research process inspired by how modern AI labs organize their research programs — treating research as an ongoing, structured effort rather than a series of one-off projects.
