Inserloft is a company building infrastructure for artificial intelligence — spanning frontier models, efficient small models, a development platform, developer tools, APIs, agents, and open research.

Inserloft's products and initiatives include:

- [Pixel Platform](/docs/platform/pixel) — the main product and AI workspace
- [Cleo](/docs/cleo/introduction) — the AI assistant at the center of the ecosystem
- [Kyro](/docs/models/kyro) — the frontier model family
- [Verty](/docs/models/verty) — the first open-source model, via [OpenGit](/docs/opengit/introduction)
- [Research](/docs/research/research-areas) — the team advancing the science behind all of the above

Inserloft's guiding philosophy — "Build the future of AI infrastructure" — is covered in more depth on the [Philosophy](/docs/getting-started/philosophy) page.
