This page summarizes how data is handled across Inserloft's products, including [Pixel](/docs/platform/pixel), [Cleo](/docs/cleo/introduction), and the [APIs](/docs/apis/authentication).

- Conversations with Cleo may be retained to support [Memory](/docs/cleo/memory), unless disabled.
- Content sent through the APIs is used to generate a response and is not used to train models without explicit opt-in.
- Files uploaded through the [Files API](/docs/apis/files-api) are private to your Organization by default.

For the full legal text, see [Terms](/docs/company/terms).
