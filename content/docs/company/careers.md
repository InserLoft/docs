Inserloft is building across research, platform engineering, and product — from training frontier models like [Kyro](/docs/models/kyro) to building the Pixel [Platform](/docs/platform/pixel) developers use every day.

The hiring process generally follows these steps:

1. **Application** — submit the role-specific application form.
2. **Agreements** — review and accept any required agreements (for example, confidentiality where relevant to the role).
3. **Acceptance** — receive and accept an offer.
4. **Confirmation email** — a confirmation is sent once an offer is accepted.
5. **Onboarding** — access to internal tools and Pixel Organizations is provisioned as part of onboarding.

Open roles span Research, Platform engineering, Developer relations, and OpenGit.
