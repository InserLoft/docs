Welcome to the Inserloft documentation.

Inserloft is a company building infrastructure for artificial intelligence. Rather than shipping a single model or a single product, Inserloft is assembling a full ecosystem: frontier models, small efficient models, a platform to build and ship AI work, developer tools, APIs, autonomous agents, and an open research program.

This documentation covers every layer of that ecosystem:

- **Platform** — Pixel, the workspace where projects, models, and teams live.
- **Models** — the Kyro frontier model family and the Verty open-weight family.
- **Cleo** — the AI assistant that sits at the center of the ecosystem.
- **APIs** — programmatic access to models, chat, embeddings, and files.
- **OpenGit** — Inserloft's open-source organization and its releases.
- **Research** — publications and the problems the research team is working on.
- **Developer** — SDKs, CLI tooling, examples, and tutorials.
- **Company** — background on Inserloft as an organization.

If you are new here, start with [What is Inserloft?](/docs/getting-started/what-is-inserloft), then read the [Philosophy](/docs/getting-started/philosophy) page to understand why the ecosystem is shaped the way it is.
