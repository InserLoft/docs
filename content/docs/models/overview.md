Inserloft maintains two primary model families, plus room for future ones:

```
Kyro
 │
 ├── Kyro 3
 ├── Kyro 3.5
 └── future versions

Verty
 │
 ├── Verty Base
 ├── Verty Instruct
 └── future variants
```

**Kyro** is the frontier family — the largest and most capable models Inserloft trains. See [Kyro](/docs/models/kyro).

**Verty** is the first open-source family, published by [OpenGit](/docs/opengit/introduction) under the Apache License 2.0. See [Verty](/docs/models/verty).

Alongside these sit the **Baby LLMs** — small, efficient models optimized to run locally rather than to top a leaderboard. See [Baby LLMs](/docs/models/baby-llms).

Every model, regardless of family, is published to the Pixel [Model Registry](/docs/platform/model-registry) with a [Model Card](/docs/models/model-cards) and tracked [Benchmarks](/docs/models/benchmarks).
