Kyro is Inserloft's family of frontier models — the largest and most capable models the company trains.

Where the Verty and Baby LLM families optimize for efficiency and local use, Kyro is built to push raw capability: reasoning, long-context understanding, coding, and general knowledge at the frontier of what Inserloft's research allows.

Current and planned releases:

- **Kyro 3** — the current generation frontier model.
- **Kyro 3.5** — planned for September 2026, representing a significant step up in scale and intelligence over Kyro 3.

Kyro models are hosted directly by Inserloft and are the primary models exposed through the [Chat API](/docs/apis/chat-api) and [Responses API](/docs/apis/responses-api). They also power [Cleo](/docs/cleo/introduction) by default.
